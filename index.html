    Breaking barriers with ASL recognition
    Bridge the Gap: ASL Recognition is a groundbreaking AI project located in Thonon-les-Bains. We are dedicated to enhancing accessibility by transforming American Sign Language (ASL) gestures into real-time translations. Our innovative tool is designed for everyone, making communication effortless and inclusive. Join us as we pave the way for a more connected world, where every gesture speaks volumes and every individual is heard.
