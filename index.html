The Story Behind ASL Recognition

Growing up, I witnessed the communication challenges faced by individuals who rely on sign language. This project is my tribute to them, aimed at bridging the gap and fostering deeper connections. During development, the biggest challenge was ensuring accurate gesture recognition. We overcame this by using a comprehensive training dataset and continuously refining our AI model.

This is a Portfolio Project for Holberton School.
